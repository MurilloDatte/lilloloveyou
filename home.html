To the Light of My Heart

In your smile, I find the sun,
A warmth that shines on everyone.
Your laughter dances, soft and true,
A melody that pulls me through.

Your touch, so gentle, calms my soul,
A love that makes my spirit whole.
With every word, with every glance,
You give my heart another chance.

Your kindness blooms, a flower rare,
A beauty beyond all compare.
You hold my dreams within your palm,
A life with you, so pure, so calm.

I love you more with every day,
In every word you choose to say.
Forever, I am yours to keep,
In waking life, and even sleep.
